sites i like

FADR // A hub of incredibly powerful music production technology that I inevitably spend hours in every time I visit. There's free stemming, song sharing, an in-browser DAW for remixing, a synth generator, and more. And it's all developed by some close friends of mine!

TWELVE MEN // The endlessly entertaining personal website of the eponymous Twelve Men. The style and formatting of TM inspired A LOT of this website's design - it won't take you long to find similarities!

ATLAS OBSCURA // The ultimate source to find interesting things to do anywhere in the world.

QUEERING THE MAP // A map of the world where anyone can anonymously drop a marker and write about their LGBTQ+ experience in that region. This site is solidarity incarnate. i got rickrolled in antarctica .

SILK // An interactive art generator that I've absentmindedly toyed with on occasion since I was in middleschool. It's basically doodling in the margins of your notebook, on steroids.
