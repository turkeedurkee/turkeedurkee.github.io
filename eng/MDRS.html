The Mission Statement:

  The Mars Desert Research Station (MDRS) is an analog Mars habitat built and operated by the Mars Society, a space advocacy and research nonprofit. Every two weeks, a new crew rotates in to live an immersive simulation Mars mission while performing geological tests, horticulture, behavioral studies, and all other manners of research.
